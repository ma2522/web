The things you must keep in mind

　　　

1.Don't go out alone at night

Coronavirus has attacked French economy.　So, the number of unemployed has also increased. Such people might be commit crimes. And, we Asians are considered tourists and can suffer damage.So, avoid going out alone at night, especially for women.

2.Even if the attitude of strangers is not so good, you don't have to worry

Many French people are very wary of new people and do not open up much to them. It is just a cultural habit, so you don't have to worry about it.

3. At the French Home Party FÊTE

In France, people who are not very close to the host also attend the party. Also, since most parties are not official, there is a possibility of drugs being used. Always be careful, especially with your own drinks. Be aware that drugs can be put in your drink when you are out of sight, and you may end up using drugs unknowingly.

My purpose for studying abroad

    Improving my French skills

    I am currently studying French at university as a second language. The reason why I chose French is that it is one of the common languages in the United Nations. I would like to work in the field of international cooperation in the future. In international cooperation, I think it is difficult to understand the other person deeply if you can only speak English. By studying French, I will be able to interact with people who cannot speak English and expand the range of people I can help. That is why I would like to study at Caen University in order to learn French.

    Challenges in my field of interest

    As I mentioned before, I would like to work in the field of international cooperation. France is known as a nation of immigrants and accepts a large number of immigrants. On the other hand, Japan has been facing the problem of not accepting enough immigrants. I would like to take a close look at the immigration policy in France and find out something that can be useful for Japan's immigration policy in the future.
